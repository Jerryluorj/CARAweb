How a Patient Experiences CARA Design

Step 1: Enrollment & Testing

Baseline tests capture your biomarkers and clinical characteristics for predictive modeling.

Step 2: Intelligent Assignment

The algorithm considers your predictive covariates and prior outcomes to assign a treatment with higher expected benefit for you.

Step 3: Treatment & Monitoring

Your response is monitored; the system updates its learning for future patients with similar profiles.

Step 4: Continuous Learning

Assignments improve over time as more data accumulate, enhancing patient-centric care and trial efficiency.
