🤔 The Traditional Approach

In most clinical trials, treatments are assigned randomly — like flipping a coin. This means some patients receive treatments that aren't ideal for their unique characteristics.

Success Rate: Only 50%

✨ The CARA Design Solution

CARA (Covariate-Adjusted Response-Adaptive) Design learns from each patient and adapts in real-time. It considers your individual characteristics and previous results to match you with the treatment most likely to help you.

Success Rate: Up to 63% — a 26% improvement!
